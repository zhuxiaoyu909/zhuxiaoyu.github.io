Bitcoin's growing energy problem

Bitcoin Electricity Consumption Comparison

The calculation from Cambridge university's Bitcoin Electricity Consumption index suggests that, in 2019, bitcoin mining consumed 68.86 terawatt hours a year of electricity. That is roughly the amount of energy consumed by Chile in 2019 (Click "Show Subgroup" button to zoom in). With high energy consumption and limited use cases, the value of bitcoin is yet to be justified.
